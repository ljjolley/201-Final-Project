Add a Task

Start adding your household tasks below. First, add a task into the task bubble. Make sure to assign the task to at least one person in the household. Then assign the task to as many as five members of your household. You can also choose how frequently the task needs to be completed.

The Happy Home Task Manager will rotate through the people assigned to the take once the task has been completed. No more having to remind them yourself!




The tasks you have added are listed below. The name of the task, the frequency, as well as all of the people who are assigned to the task will be listed. When a task is first added it will be red to indicate it isn't completed yet. It will also be red on the 'Your Tasks' page. Once the task has been completed, click the task's name to turn it green.
